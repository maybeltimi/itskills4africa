KNOW ABOUT US

We are a registered non-governmental organization

Passionate and committed to raising innovative champions
By pacesetting the global educational standards through Information Technology skills, availing Leadership and Career Mentorship to all Africans as well as mobilizing resources to promote sustainable growth and development in Africa.
OUR MISSION
Promoting integrity, empowerment, & committed to excellence.
To empower African teeming youths with skills in digital and related vocational studies with the drive to deliver innovative manpower to the nation and global market
OUR VISION
Empower and provide a platform that enhances growth.
Our mandate is to raise innovative Africans who will revolutionize Africa through pacesetting the global educational standards and maximizing the advantage of technology.
